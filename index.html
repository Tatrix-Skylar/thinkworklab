About Think Work Lab

Think Work Lab is a research and intelligence platform focused on the future of work and human resources.

We help organizations understand trends, interpret transformations, and make better strategic decisions about people, leadership, and organizational culture.

Through research, editorial content, awards, and advisory services, Think Work Lab connects data, ideas, and practice to advance innovation in HR.

  • Market intelligence and trend analysis
  • Custom research projects for organizations

Specialized Content and Editorial Services

  • Curation of institutional websites and knowledge hubs

Events and Training

  • Training for sales and marketing teams on how to communicate with HR audiences
  • Strategic advisory for engagement with HR leaders

Innovations

Think Work Innovations is our platform dedicated to elevating, connecting, and accelerating HR innovation across organizations and leadership communities.

  • Think Work Innovations Award: recognizing the most innovative HR practices.
  • Innovation training and education: enabling HR teams to build capabilities for continuous transformation.
  • Knowledge platform: connecting innovative organizations and leaders through research-informed exchange.
